Language coverage, honestly

Translations are produced by Claude Fable 5 (Anthropic) with automatic backup from GPT-4o (OpenAI), two of the most capable AI systems available. This page shows plainly what those systems can and cannot deliver today. Every delivery is checked by a second AI and scored; nothing here is promised, it is measured.

LanguageTextSpoken aloudModel quality

Text: available in every listed language. Each translation is checked by a second AI and scored.

Spoken aloud: device speech voices do not exist for every language. Where a voice is missing, spoken audio falls back to captions rather than failing silently. This is a gap in today's platforms, not a settings problem.

Sign languages: the largest gap in every frontier model. No AI today produces usable sign-language video. American Sign Language is delivered as text gloss, and video is being developed with Deaf-led evaluation, because only Deaf raters can judge it.

Emerging: works today; have a person verify important messages. For crisis, legal, and medical moments, the platform routes to humans by policy.
